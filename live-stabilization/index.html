When you need to leave Stabilization

Looking ahead

Some things take a long time to prepare. Start before you are forced to. Here is what to keep an eye on from this sub-phase, and roughly when to begin.

Stabilisation ends when the service is steady. Two things can move it on.

  • Forward to Growth,

    when the service is steady and the work turns to extending it.

  • Back to a rebuild,

    when stabilisation cannot settle the service and a deeper fix or replatforming is needed.
